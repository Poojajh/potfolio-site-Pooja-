Introduction

Wagtales is a first of its kind location based app for pet parents. Users can find anything related to pets from cafes to vets, shops to emergency services in any area they choose.

What is Wagtales

Wagtales is a location app for pet parents in which users can search any kind of establishment for their pets and read/leave reviews as well. Being a pet parent myself, I saw something missing in this field which is very important. Wagtales helps users find everything they might need for their best friend in any area
